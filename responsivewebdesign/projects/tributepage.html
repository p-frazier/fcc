LINK

The Child Hero of Hyrule



Link is the humble but brave protagonist of the immensely popular series of video games released under the The Legend of Zelda® franchise developed by Nintendo™ in Japan. He was first introduced in the 1986 classic The Legend of Zelda released for the Nintendo Entertainment system. Since then, Link has appeared in 17 additional video games titles across 8 major console platforms as well as several handheld devices.
