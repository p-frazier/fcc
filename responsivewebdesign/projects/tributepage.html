The Legend of Zelda

Acclaimed Game Franchise

Link is the humble but brave protagonist of the immensely popular series of video games released under the The Legend of Zelda® franchise developed by Nintendo™ in Japan. He was first introduced in the 1986 classic The Legend of Zelda released for the Nintendo Entertainment system.
